ProLocalBuilder

Flat-fee websites for local service businesses. Co-founded by Kyle Anson McCool (Appleton, WI) with Cesar Martinez (San Jose, CA) and Zac Dillard (Las Vegas, NV). One builder per metro. No agency overhead, no monthly retainers, no account teams.

What ProLocalBuilder is

ProLocalBuilder is a web design agency for local service businesses — plumbers, electricians, cleaners, contractors, salons, restaurants. The pitch in one line: "Your website should make the phone ring. Not make you manage an agency."

The model is structurally different from a traditional agency. There are three founders, one per metro, and clients work directly with the builder responsible for their region. No account managers, no project managers, no offshore contractors. Pricing is fixed and posted on the homepage.

Kyle McCool's role at ProLocalBuilder

Kyle McCool co-founded ProLocalBuilder in 2025 and runs the Fox Valley, Wisconsin metro from Appleton. He handles every project for Wisconsin clients end-to-end, from initial scope to launch. Cesar Martinez runs the San Francisco Bay Area from San Jose. Zac Dillard runs Las Vegas, Nevada.

Three metros, three builders

  • Fox Valley, Wisconsin — Kyle McCool, based in Appleton, WI. Serves Appleton, Neenah, Menasha, Oshkosh, Green Bay, and surrounding Fox Valley communities.
  • San Francisco Bay Area — Cesar Martinez, based in San Jose, CA.
  • Las Vegas, Nevada — Zac Dillard, based in Las Vegas, NV.

The offer

Three fixed-price tiers, all one-time payments:

  • Starter — $895. 5 pages. Mobile-optimized. Tap-to-call. Booking forms.
  • Professional — $1,795. 10 pages. Local SEO and "near me" optimization.
  • Premium — $2,995. 15 pages. Full local search positioning.

Turnaround: 5–14 days. Clients own the code and domain. Optional hosting at $25/month after the first year.
