FAQ

Pricing questions

Does Phantomline require a subscription?

No. Phantomline offers a free tier, monthly and yearly Creator Pro plans, a Studio plan, and a one-time Founding Lifetime tier. The local AI pipeline does not charge per render. There are no usage-based API fees.

What is the founding lifetime tier?

A one-time payment that unlocks Creator Pro permanently for the first 500 buyers. Lifetime updates included.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Monthly and yearly Creator Pro and Studio plans cancel any time. Founding Lifetime is a single payment with no recurring charge.

Is Phantomline an alternative to Submagic, OpusClip, or Pictory?

Phantomline is a local-first alternative for creators who want private rendering, no per-render API costs, and a faceless YouTube workflow under one roof. Cloud-only tools have their place; Phantomline is the pick if you'd rather pay once and run everything on your own hardware.
