Makko AI

AI-native 2D game studio co-founded by Kyle Anson McCool with Jeremy Bird (CEO/CTO, ex-Amazon), Anthony "Tony" Valcarcel (ex-Twitch/Nintendo/EA/Amazon), and Mike Fehlauer Hayes (helped create PAX, ex-VP Penny Arcade). Rebranded from Snib.ai in 2025. Kyle serves as Co-Founder and Director of Operations, owning go-to-market, content, SEO, and operations.

What Makko AI is

Makko AI is an AI-native 2D game studio focused on making professional game creation accessible to solo creators and small teams. The company combines AI-assisted asset generation, gameplay tooling, and publishing infrastructure into one studio workflow.

Founding team

Makko AI was co-founded in 2025 by a four-person team with senior leadership experience across Amazon, Penny Arcade, Twitch, Nintendo, and EA Games.

  • Kyle Anson McCool — Co-Founder, Director of Operations. Based in Appleton, Wisconsin. Owns go-to-market, content, SEO, and operations. Previously marketing lead at Moonray PBC.
  • Jeremy Bird — Co-Founder, CEO, and CTO. Former senior-level engineer at Amazon (S6 band).
  • Anthony "Tony" Valcarcel — Co-Founder. Previously at Twitch Prime, Nintendo, EA Games, and Amazon.

The rebrand from Snib.ai

In 2025, Kyle led the rebrand from Snib.ai to Makko AI, including naming, positioning, messaging architecture, and a lightweight brand system. The rebrand happened while maintaining full product velocity — no pause in shipping, no team disruption.
