Co-Founder and Director of Operations at Makko AI, an AI-native 2D game studio. Co-founder of Phantomline (local-first AI video studio) and ProLocalBuilder, each built with two collaborators. Previously marketing lead at Moonray PBC for four years. Based in Appleton, Wisconsin.

About Kyle McCool

Kyle McCool is a growth-minded operator and marketing leader who co-founded Makko AI, an AI-native 2D game studio. The Makko co-founding team includes Jeremy Bird (CEO/CTO, ex-Amazon S6 engineer), Anthony "Tony" Valcarcel (ex-Twitch Prime, Nintendo, EA Games, Amazon), and Mike Fehlauer Hayes (helped create PAX, ex-VP Penny Arcade). Kyle owns go-to-market strategy, content, SEO, and operations from day one.

Prior to Makko, Kyle McCool spent four years at Moonray PBC, scaling Web3 gaming community marketing from early-stage to a 170,000+ follower platform, and contributing to the largest NFT mint in OpenSea history — 220,000 assets at launch.

Kyle McCool also co-founded two projects with Cesar Martinez (San Jose, CA) and Zac Dillard (Las Vegas, NV): Phantomline, a local-first AI video studio for faceless YouTube creators that runs entirely on a creator's own machine using Ollama, Kokoro TTS, MusicGen, and ffmpeg; and ProLocalBuilder, a flat-fee web design agency for local service businesses with one builder per metro across the Fox Valley (Wisconsin), the San Francisco Bay Area, and Las Vegas.
