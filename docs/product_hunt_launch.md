## Tactical reminders for launch day

- **Reply to comments within 2 hours, no exceptions.** PH's algorithm
  weighs comment velocity nearly as much as upvotes.
- **Don't ask for upvotes in comments.** It's against PH ToS and gets
  flagged. Pre-launch DM is fine; on-platform asks are not.
- **Pin the demo video to your first comment.** ~80% of PH visitors
  won't leave the comment thread to watch a video on another tab.
- **Track conversions** — install Plausible or PostHog on the landing
  page tagged with `?ref=ph` so you know exactly what converted.
- **Sleep before launch night.** First-hour velocity matters; if you're
  awake until 3am futzing with copy you'll be useless when comments
  start landing at 6am PT.
